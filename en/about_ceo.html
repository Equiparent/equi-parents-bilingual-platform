Biography

Javiera Rojas Cowper - CEO and Founder of Equi-Parents

My journey of over 15 years as a school teacher and a co-parent has been key to deeply understanding the needs and challenges of our users. This experience has given me a solid innovative vision and execution capacity, essential elements for the takeoff and development of the startup.

My 100% dedication to software development and the company has been the driving force behind its progress. I have personally led the IT development of the SaaS web platform, demonstrating high learning abilities, resilience, and proactivity in overcoming delays in funding, starting a computer programming career, and managing a company at the same time. This human and social capital is fundamental for the growth and sustainability of Equi-Parents.
