Frequent Asked Questions EquiParents

Key concepts about the web services platform for separated parents.

What is the Mission of Equi-Parents?
To facilitate navigation through the turbulent waters of separated parenting.
What is the main Objective of Equi-Parents?
To facilitate the organization of equal custody for separated moms and dads.
What is the main Problem that Equi-Parents solves?
Discussions and complications in organizing schedules, expenses, and communication in shared parenting.
What is the functionality: Shared Calendar?
Allows organizing days or weeks of the year with distinctive colors for each parent, facilitating the visualization of custody.
What is the functionality: Expense Management?
Allows for transparent tracking of child-rearing expenses and maintenance.
What is the functionality: Notifications?
Reminds about commitments, custody shifts, and alerts if there are any changes in the calendar.
What is the functionality: Online Mediation?
Offers a direct link to schedule an online mediation session; a free space to help resolve conflicts that may arise between parents.
What is the alternating weeks custody arrangement?
Children spend a full week with one parent and the following week with the other.
What is the benefit of the alternating weeks system?
Allows both parents to spend quality time with the children, share activities, and help with homework.
What are the requirements for the efficient functioning of custody arrangements?
It is ideal, but not exclusive, for both parents to use the platform to ensure everyone is on the same page.
Is there a solution for parents' communication with new partners?
By centralizing parents' communication on the platform, it avoids calls or direct messages that could cause conflicts with new partners.
Does the Equi-Parents web platform provide Summaries, Statistics, or Reports?
Yes. Equi-Parents provides a monthly summary with relevant information about the children's progress and achievements, with the option to download them.
